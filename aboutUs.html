About Us

rocket_launch

Leapfrog is started with a simple goal: to simplify the way you prepare for Limited Departmental Competitive Exam for SO grade. We have noticed that while quizzes are available, they are scattered, making it difficult for people to practice what they had learned.

Many resort to taking unnecessary printouts, which can only be used once. We wanted to create an ecosystem where you could focus solely on your preparation without any distractions.

That's where the Leapfrog series comes in. We offer a streamlined platform that makes it easy to access and practice quizzes in one convenient location. Our quizzes are designed to help you test your knowledge and reinforce what you've learned, so you can feel confident and prepared for your exam.
